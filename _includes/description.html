In the aftermath of the 2016 election, many people have a renewed interest in social justice, inclusion, and equality in our community. It has been said that now is the time to find an organization to get involved in, and if you can't find one - start one! It turns out we have many organizations right here in our community doing good work.

Holland Loves is designed to be a resource for our community. Please use this site to get in touch with organizations with whom to serve, advocate for, and support financially.
